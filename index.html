About Resham Singh Anmol

Born in Nakatpur (Ambala), Resham Singh Anmol is one of the most loved Punjabi live singers known for his iconic songs like Nagni, Taur, Chete Karda, and Bhabhi End. His electrifying stage presence, folk roots, and energetic performance style make him a top choice for weddings, festivals, corporate events, and music concerts.

With years of experience and millions of loyal listeners across the globe, Resham Singh Anmol continues to deliver unforgettable Punjabi live shows full of power-packed vocals and desi energy.
